The Mathematics

The Fibonacci Word sequence is generated by concatenation, similar to the Fibonacci numbers but with strings. Starting with F(1) = "1" and F(2) = "0", each subsequent word is formed by concatenating the previous two: F(n) = F(n-1) + F(n-2).
